Services

Area Rug Cleaning

At Houston Area Rug Cleaning, we provide an extensive range of methods and techniques for area rug cleaning. Our technicians are experts at cleaning area rugs and restoring them to a pristine condition. Our area rug cleaning processes have been designed, factoring in all the variables that may impact any area rug's softness, color, texture, and appearance.

Generally Area Rugs are placed in high traffic areas, so they naturally tend to gather the brunt of foot traffic, and therefore dry soil. All area rugs, but wool rugs in particular can carry up to 150% of its weight in dry soil alone, before even appearing soiled. Each Area Rug Cleaning begins with dirt and soil removal through the use of a dusting platform to ensure maximum dry soil removal before the rug is placed in our area rug bath.
